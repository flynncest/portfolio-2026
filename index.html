Background

I'm Yap Yung Hui, a founder advisor based in the Netherlands. I work with early-stage founders who have a strong idea but struggle to communicate it clearly enough to attract their first paying customers.

My work lives at the intersection of strategy and storytelling — finding the signal in a founder's vision and shaping it into something the market responds to.

Before advising, I spent years working inside startups and watching the same pattern repeat: smart founders with real solutions failing to get traction simply because their positioning was off. That's the problem I solve.
